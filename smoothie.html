Mango Smoothie Recipe

Total Time: 5 Mins

Ingredients

  • 2 Fresh Mangos or 2 Cups Frozen Mangos
  • 1 Frozen Banana (or half of a large banana), you can use use frozen if using frozen mango.
  • 1/2 Cup Milk, dairy or dairy-free
  • 1/2 Cup Yogurt, dairy or dairy-free

Directions

  1. Add all ingredients to a high-powered blender and blend until creamy.
